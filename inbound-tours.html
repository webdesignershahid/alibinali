Half Day City Tour

PACKAGE DETAILS

Our Chauffeur/Guide will pick you up from any pick up point within Doha. You will first head to the old Dhow Harbour, which is also called the Al Bandar area, where you will see many traditional wooden boats picturesquely docked, from here you will see the beautiful West Bay skyline. Thereafter, you will proceed to the Museum of Islamic Art, where you can witness manuscripts, textiles, ceramics, and other works. The museum, has emerged as one of the world's most encyclopedic collections of Islamic art. Then, you will visit the Gold Souq, where you can enjoy a walk around the glittering shops that offer jewellery with luxury designs and amazing craftsmanship. Your next stop is called Souq Waqif, a traditional market where you can shop, dine and explore. Here, you will find lots of shops selling spices, fabrics, perfumes, traditional handicraft products and souvenirs, as well as a stable for Arabian horses and camels. Afterward, you will head to The Pearl Qatar - a luxurious man-made island, featuring Mediterranean-style marinas, with famous restaurants and coffee shops. The last stop will be at Katara Cultural Village, a cultural beacon, a lighthouse of art, radiating in the Middle East through theatre, literature, music, visual art, conventions and exhibitions. The location is also famous for its Blue Mosque and Pigeon Towers.

Package Inclusive of:
  • Pick-up and drop-off within Doha city limits.
  • Transportation in an air-conditioned vehicle.
  • Brief stops at Dhow Harbour and the Museum of Islamic Art.
  • Visit to the Souq Waqif.
  • Visit to The Pearl Qatar.
  • Visit to Katara Cultural Village.
  • All guides are licensed and English-speaking.

Timings:

AM Tour - 9.00 AM to 1.00 PM

PM Tour - 2.00 PM to 6.00 PM

City Tour by Night with Dinner

PACKAGE DETAILS

We will pick you up from the airport/hotel in Doha and drive you through Corniche, where you will be fascinated by the new, vibrant look of Doha with its modern towers and traditional Dhow boats decorated with colorful lights by night. Your first stop will be the old Dhow Harbour, where you will see the beautiful West Bay skyline with its gleaming buildings at night. Thereafter, you will visit the Museum of Islamic Art, designed by Chinese-American architect I.M Pei. This geometric-shaped structure boasts a large collection of Islamic Art. You will then head straight to Souq Waqif, a traditional market with many shops for knick-knacks, spices, fabrics, perfumes, and traditional handicraft products. Adjoining Souq Waqif is the stable for Arabian horses and camels as well as a Falcon Souq. You will then proceed to Katara Cultural Village, a cultural beacon with an amphitheater, planetarium, art galleries’ and restaurants in the middle of the city. Your last stop will be at The Pearl Qatar; here you can find splendid marinas with luxury boats docked, World Famous restaurants, high-end branded shops, Luxury car showrooms, Coffee shops, and Residential apartments.

Package Inclusive of:
  • Pick-up and drop-off within Doha City limits.
  • Transportation in an air-conditioned vehicle.
  • Brief stops at Dhow Harbour and the Museum of Islamic Art.
  • Visit Souq Waqif.
  • Visit The Pearl Qatar.
  • Visit Katara Cultural Village.
  • Dinner at a local restaurant.
  • All guides are licensed and English-speaking.

Note

  • Tour will be on a shared basis.

Timings:

PM Tour - 6.00 PM to 10.00 PM

Panoramic City Tour

PACKAGE DETAILS

Upon your arrival at Hamad International Airport, we will take you on a short tour, around the beautiful City of Doha. We will drive you along the Corniche, which is approximately a 7 km-long waterfront path with many traditional wooden Dhow boats parked alongside it. Across is the beautiful Doha skyline with its exceptional architectural towers. You will then head straight to The Pearl Qatar, which is a man-made island created in the shape of an oyster. Here you will see luxury towers and villas, high-end shopping, hotels, and fine dining restaurants. Next, you will pass by the Katara Cultural Village, which is the largest multicultural cultural project in Qatar with beautiful theatres, concert halls, exhibition galleries, and cutting-edge facilities. Katara is also famous for its Blue Mosque and Pigeon Towers. From there we will drive you to Souq Waqif, a heritage market that features traditional Qatari architecture, handicrafts, souvenirs, and spices from around the world. After a brief tour around the city, you will be dropped off back at the airport or at your hotel.

  • Pick-up and drop-off within Doha city limits.
  • Transportation in an air-conditioned vehicle.
  • Brief stops at Dhow Harbour and the Museum of Islamic Art.
  • Visit to the Souq Waqif.
  • Visit to The Pearl Qatar.
  • Visit to Katara Cultural Village.
  • All guides are licensed and English-speaking.

AM Tour - 9.00 AM to 1.00 PM

24 Hour Hop on - Hop off Bus Tour

PACKAGE DETAILS

Enjoy the city of Doha at your own pace on our Hop On - Hop Off Sightseeing Tour. Our double-decker buses run every 30 - 40 minutes with stops at all major city attractions such as Souq Waqif, Katara Cultural Village, The Pearl Qatar, and the Museum of Islamic Art. We also pass by some of the main hotels in Doha which are Marriott, Sharq Village and Spa, Sheraton, and Hilton Hotel. This tour allows you to set the pace, taking your own time at each stop according to your liking. The Hop on - Hop off ticket is valid for 24 hours, once the ticket is redeemed with unlimited rides around the city. You can hop on at our main, branded, bus stops at all major city attractions and at kiosks located at Souq Waqif, Sheraton Park, and Katara Cultural Village. When you purchase this tour, you will receive free access to our night tour which is a one-hour relaxing drive around the city where you will get to see beautiful landmarks. Audio guides are available in multiple languages onboard vehicles.

Package Inclusive of:
  • Pick-up and drop-off at any Hop On - Hop Off bus stops.
  • Souq Waqif
  • Sheraton Park
  • Katara Cultural Village
  • Redeem your Hop On-Hop Off voucher at any of the kiosks.
  • Multi-language audio guides on board vehicles in Arabic, English, German, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Portuguese (Brazilian).
  • Buses pass by and make a stop at the Hop On - Hop Off route stops every 30 - 40 minutes.
  • Tickets are valid for 24 hours with Night City Tour access for 1 hour at 19:00 starting from Souq Waqif.

Timings:

From 16 September until 30 April: Day Tour - 09:00 - 17:00 | Night City Tour - 19:00 - 20:00

From 1 May until 15 September: Evening Tour - 16:00 - 22:00 | Night City Tour: 22:00 - 23:00
